Lore

In the vastness of space, humans have built a huge civilization that spreads across many planets. Earth is the center of it all, where leaders make important decisions. But there's trouble brewing.

A dangerous sickness is spreading everywhere. It doesn't care if you're on a distant planet or in the heart of Earth's cities. People are scared and looking for help.

They're turning to Earth and other strongholds for support. Scientists are working hard to find a cure, and doctors are risking their lives to help the sick. But it's tough. Supplies are running low, and some places are fighting over who gets what.

Even with all the problems, there are stories of bravery and kindness. Regular folks are stepping up to help, doing whatever they can to make things better.

As time goes on, things are getting harder. But people are holding on to hope. They know that as long as they stick together and keep trying, they can get through anything.


The Zoomies

Introducing Zoomies, your one-stop solution for seamless buying and selling of goods across the galaxy. Founded on the principle of connecting sellers and buyers in a hassle-free manner, Zoomies revolutionizes the way commerce is conducted in the intergalactic civilization.

Zoomies acts as the intermediary between sellers and buyers, providing a convenient platform for transactions to take place. Sellers can easily list their products, whether they be fresh produce from distant agricultural colonies, rare artifacts from remote planets, or cutting-edge technology from advanced research facilities. With Zoomies, sellers gain access to a vast network of potential buyers, expanding their reach beyond the confines of their own planetary borders.

Buyers, on the other hand, benefit from a diverse marketplace where they can browse through a wide array of goods from the comfort of their own homes. Whether they seek exotic delicacies from far-off worlds, traditional crafts from ancient civilizations, or state-of-the-art gadgets from cutting-edge manufacturers, buyers can find it all on Zoomies.

But Zoomies doesn't stop at just facilitating transactions. The company goes the extra mile by handling all aspects of shipment and delivery. Leveraging advanced logistics networks and state-of-the-art transportation technologies, Zoomies ensures that goods are swiftly and safely transported from seller to buyer, no matter the distance.

With Zoomies, sellers can focus on what they do best – creating and producing high-quality goods – without worrying about the complexities of distribution and delivery. Meanwhile, buyers can shop with confidence, knowing that their purchases will arrive promptly and intact, thanks to Zoomies' meticulous attention to detail.

In a galaxy where distance is no longer a barrier to commerce, Zoomies stands as a beacon of efficiency and reliability, bridging the gap between sellers and buyers with unparalleled convenience and ease. Join the Zoomies community today and experience the future of intergalactic trade firsthand.

Development

Hypertext Markup Language(HTML5) and Cascading Style Sheets(CSS3) are used for structuring and beautification of the webpages. Javascript is used to create more complicated animations and manipulate DOM and also facilitate better form validation. All animations have been developed to look futuristic in a tablet like view. The code has been tested on Google Chrome on screens ranging from 13.6inch to 17.6inch screens. Mobile support has not yet been implemented. Bootstrap CSS has been used for easier implementation and JQuery has been used for easier JS implementations. GitHub has been used for version control while github pages is used for website hosting.

Artwork

Many custom planets have been digitally drawn by Aarushi Dharna (Team member) to add originality and concept while also immersing us as developers more into the project. A detailed description of each planet is provided in the fun info along with random facts and quirks about them.
